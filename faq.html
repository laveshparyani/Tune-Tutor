Frequently Asked Questions
What is Tune Tutor?
Tune Tutor offers live, one-on-one online music classes with professional tutors in various languages and musical styles.
How do I book a class?
You can book a class directly through our website by selecting your preferred tutor and scheduling a session.
What instruments do you offer lessons for?
We offer lessons for a wide range of instruments including guitar, piano, violin, drums, and more.
Are the classes tailored to individual needs?
Yes, our classes are customized based on your learning preferences and goals.
How do I contact my tutor?
You can contact your tutor through the messaging system on our platform.
What languages are the classes available in?
Classes are available in English and major Indian languages.
Can I try a demo class before booking?
Yes, we offer free demo classes to experience our teaching methods.
What if I miss a class?
You can reschedule missed classes through our platform.
How are tutors selected?
Tutors are selected through a rigorous interview process and must have professional music qualifications.
Can I choose my tutor?
Yes, you can choose your tutor based on their profile and expertise.
Are there any prerequisites for taking a class?
There are no specific prerequisites; our classes cater to all skill levels.
What equipment do I need for online classes?
You need a reliable internet connection, a computer or tablet, and the instrument you wish to learn.
